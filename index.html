The Secret Success Trap Keeping You Stuck

You're driven, you're ambitious. You've put the hours in, earned the salary, enjoyed the perks. So why do you feel adrift? The Success Trap is you were told happiness is hitting 50k, then 100k, then 500k. Getting top biller. You believed this would feel incredible. It can bring more stress than it solves. You hit your targets and NOTHING.

You then set a BIGGER target, believing this one will be different, but doing more of the same gets you more of the same.

  • You've set yourself the targets, worked yourself to the bone, hit your goal and felt nothing (so why keep trying?)
  • No matter what you earn there's a feeling of 'this isn't enough' which allows you to be top performer in business, but it can (and will) have a huge impact on your personal life
  • You've built the business, but can't enjoy it because if you switch off, even for a week, the whole thing will fall apart.
  • What was a goal in the early part of your career is now impacting your peace of mind which in turn is making you less effective at work.
  • You constantly beat yourself for how you feel, because to anyone looking in from the outside, you 'have it all'

"The irony of the Secret Success Trap is your drive and ambition got you here; and the same drive and ambition is preventing you from enjoying it." - Shaun Doherty.

The 'keep adding more' approach of most coaching implies when you do more, you'll find the Peace you're looking for. A better morning routine, 10,000 steps. Mantras in the mirror. Where this approach fails is when the goal is to feel lighter; adding more won't cut it. The secret of the Peace Power and Performance System is when you strip things back, you unshackle yourself to do your best work yet. All while really enjoying the process.

The Inside-Out Approach

Change Your Story. Change your Results. Keep your Drive and Ambition. Amplify your Peace. Discover your Power. Accelerate your Performance.

The Principles behind the Peace Power and Performance System point to one truth lost in the drive for more. Your Clarity, your presence, your focus, calm and happiness are never outside you.

You are the creator of your experience. When you live from this truth, it frees you from the belief 'more stuff = more happiness'. This does not mean you drop your ambition or lower your goals, but you approach them from a more sustainable place.

This allows you to turn up at work or at home without an external story holding your happiness hostage, behind the next target.

The Framework

The Peace, Power, and Performance Framework.

Everything changes from the inside out.

01

Peace

Peace is two-fold. One, it's the driver of all your actions. Everything you do as a human, at some level, is built on the belief you'll experience more peace for doing it. Two, Peace is your natural state. Not something you have to earn, and not something which needs to be outsourced to external goals.

02

Power

Power is when you understand the true nature of your experience. No event has the power to make you feel anything; it is your thinking about the event which causes your feelings about it. This puts your power with you. Not with external happenings.

03

Performance

When your Peace and Power are in alignment, the Performance takes care of itself. The difference being, instead of you having to push towards goals, targets and growth, you are pulled there. In short, you use a much more efficient and sustainable fuel source for your goals.

What You Might Be Wondering

What is Principles-Based coaching, how is it different?

Most coaching works on behaviour, habits, or mindset, what you do and how you think. The Peace Power and Performance System works up steam of that. It points to the fundamental nature of how your thought creates experience. When you begin to understand this, change happens naturally and without the usual effort because you're addressing the source, not the symptoms.

Do I need to be in crisis or struggling to benefit from this?

No. Many clients are objectively successful and functioning well. But there's usually a recognition that something is missing, or that they're operating from a place of pressure rather than Peace. If this sounds familiar, the work is relevant regardless of how things look from the outside.

How are sessions delivered?

Sessions are delivered on Google Meet, typically bi-weekly, across six months. They're conversational in nature, not structured presentations or worksheets. The work comes from the conversations and what you bring to each call.
